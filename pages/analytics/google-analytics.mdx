# Setting Up Google Analytics

Follow these steps to integrate Google Analytics.

## Step 1: Create a Google Analytics Account

1. Go to the [Google Analytics](https://analytics.google.com/) website.
2. Sign in with your Google account and create a new property for your website.

- In the left-hand menu, navigate to **Admin** > **+ Create** and select **Property**.

3. You'll receive a **Measurement ID** (e.g., `G-XXXXXXXXXX`).
