# Setting Up Customer Support with Crisp

Follow these steps to integrate the Crisp live chat feature into your application.

## Step 1: Create a Crisp Account

1. Go to the [Crisp website](https://crisp.chat/) and sign up for an account.
2. Once logged in, navigate to your website settings and copy your **Crisp Website ID**.

## Step 2: Add the Crisp ID to `.env.local`

1. Open your `.env.local` file and add the following environment variable:
   ```env
   NEXT_PUBLIC_CRISP_ID=YOUR_CRISP_WEBSITE_ID
